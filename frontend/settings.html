About Your Settings

Single-User Demo

SweatLogic runs in single-user demonstration mode. Your settings are saved to browser local storage and do not require an account.

What Gets Saved

Display name, measurement units, workout goal, and experience level are saved locally and used by the workout builder.

Data Privacy

All data stays in your browser. Nothing is sent to external services. Clearing browser data will reset your settings.
